About Smart Resume Analyzer

The Smart Resume Analyzer is a modern B.Tech mini project designed to bridge the gap between education and industry expectations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students and job seekers with a data-driven tool that helps them understand their technical readiness for various job roles. By providing instant, actionable feedback, we aim to accelerate skill development and career growth.

How It Works

We use advanced PDF text extraction technology combined with role-specific skill mapping algorithms. The system analyzes your resume content, compares it against industry standards for your selected role, and generates a comprehensive readiness score and learning path.

Key Technologies
Java 17+ Spring Boot 3 Thymeleaf Spring Security OAuth2 H2 Database Apache PDFBox Bootstrap 5
